A Love Story That Became a Business

It started with a wedding — two people who fell in love over shared dreams of building something meaningful together. What began as pillow talk about the future evolved into Streamlined, a family business born from the belief that work should flow as naturally as life itself.

We noticed something simple yet profound: brilliant people were drowning in busywork, innovative companies were struggling with outdated tools, and everyone seemed to be working harder, not smarter. There had to be a better way.

So we built one. Today, Streamlined helps you move through work and life with the same grace we discovered in our partnership. We've organized our offerings into three interconnected realms, each designed to remove friction from your journey:

Talent brings you AI-enhanced assistants who think, adapt, and grow with your needs. Like having a brilliant colleague who never sleeps.

Interfaces transforms how you work with tools that feel like extensions of your mind — intuitive, powerful, and surprisingly delightful.

Resources curates the wisdom you need to navigate career transitions, industry insights, and life's bigger questions.

Everything we create carries the DNA of our origin story: human connection enhanced by intelligent technology. Not AI for AI's sake, but thoughtful augmentation that helps you focus on what matters most.
